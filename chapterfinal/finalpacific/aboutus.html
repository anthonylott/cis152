Our Mission

Pacific Trails Resort's mission is to provide quality and affordable outdoor adventures in a beautiful ocean view setting. Our dedicated and experienced team is ready to guide you on your journey during your stay, ensuring all activities are safe and enjoyable.

Pacific Trails Resort wants to ensure that you are able to enjoy and relax surrounded bu nature, wheather that be enjoying our outdoor pool, hiking through our paths with your guide or family, or enjoying complementary meals and beverages at our lodge. We have a lot of different activities available for our guests of all ages.

Our Activities Include:

  • Mountian Biking
  • Bird Watching
  • Hiking/Backpacking
  • Kayaking and Canoeing
  • Outdoor Yoga
  • Swimming Pools
  • Rock Climbing
    • and so much more!

Come Visit Us

We're a hiking, outdoor nature-loving adventurous local family and we founded Pacific Trails Resort after our honeymoon nearby. Our mission is to make the outdoors accessible to all families everywhere and preserve the natural beauty of our oceans and forest. Hope to see you soon!
